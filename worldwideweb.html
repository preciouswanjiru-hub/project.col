World Wide Web

The World Wide Web (WWW) is a global system of interconnected webpages that is hypertext documents and multimedia that are accessible via the internet

Roles of the World Wide Web

  1. Information Sharing and access- Enables sharing of data, news, research and educational content on a global scale
  2. Communication and connectivity- Facilitates global communication via email, social media and video conferencing.
  3. E-commerce- Creates a platform for online buying and selling, marketing and new business models
  4. Information organization- Uses hypertext to link information in a manner that mirrors human thought processes
  5. Navigation and retreival- Uses web browsers to transform readable content thus a user- friendly interface
